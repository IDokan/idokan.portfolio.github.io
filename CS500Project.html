CS500 Project

In Digipen Junior year, I made ray tracing project.

The first milestone, I implemented intersections between ray and objects, such as sphere, cylinder, triangle, and box.

The second milestone, I added ray-tracing functions. Upper image is a result of 4096 passes. There are an implicit lighting that calculates diffuse colors of rays, and an explicit lighting that calculates possibility of the portion is exposed by lightings.

The third milestone, I added reflection functions. It is a harmony among surface distribution, fresnel reflection, and self-shadow.
